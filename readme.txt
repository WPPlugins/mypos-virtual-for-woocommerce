== Description ==
myPOS Checkout is a WooCommerce payment gateway extension especially designed for European-based merchants who need a simple and secure complete checkout solution. Start accepting credit and debit card payments at your online store in few easy steps.
By using myPOS Checkout you enjoy a checkout extension backed-up by a comprehensive free merchant account with multiple currencies.

You will also need to register for myPOS account at https://www.mypos.eu.

Should you have any questions, please contact us at online@mypos.eu.


== Installation ==
= Installation =

1. Sign in to your WordPress site as an administrator
2. From your WordPress dashboard go to Plugins > Add New
3. Search for myPOS Checkout plugin
4. Click on 'Install Now' button to install myPOS Checkout plugin
5. Activate the plugin by clicking on 'Activate Plugin'
6. Once the plugin is activated it is visible under menu Installed Plugins


= Configuration =

To configure the plugin, go to WooCommerce > Settings > Checkout > myPOS Checkout. By clicking on myPOS Checkout you can start to configure the payment method.

= Requirements =

* The minimum PHP version required for the myPOS Checkout plugin is 5.4.8.
* In order to process card transactions successfully myPOS Checkout requires secure connection by using HTTPS protocol. You should install an SSL certificate onto your website to initiate a secure session. If your website is not using SSL certificate, your transactions will be automatically reversed by the system.


Once myPOS Checkout is installed and configured, your customers will be able to select it as a payment method and pay for your goods and services easily and secure using their Debit/Credit card.

For further information please refer to https://www.mypos.eu.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= What is myPOS? =

myPOS is a cutting-edge card payment solution that helps European-based businesses to accept cashless payments by providing them with a simple, yet secure card payment processing services. Our range of payment products allows merchants to process cashless transactions both at physical retail locations and online.

= What is a myPOS Account? =

myPOS Account is a free merchant account that gives you a comprehensive online banking experience at no subscription or service costs. All the money processed through myPOS is settled into the myPOS Account. It comes with a free business prepaid Mastercard and IBAN, linked to the account. You can safely keep your generated revenue in your myPOS Account, transfer it via outgoing bank transfer or use your associated Mastercard to withdraw cash from ATMs. To find more about myPOS Merchant Account, please visit https://www.mypos.eu/en/merchant-account.

= Why should I open a myPOS Account? =

You have to open a myPOS Account in order to use the myPOS Checkout plugin. All money processed through the myPOS Checkout payments gateway will be instantly available in your myPOS Account.
Your company must be headquartered in a EU or EEA country.

= How to open a myPOS Account? =

1. Go to https://www.mypos.eu;
2. Click on Login > Sign Up to register for myPOS account and follow the steps;
Please note that you are able to accept payments immediately after registration. However, until account verification your account is Basic and there are transaction limits.

= Where can I find settings needed for test environment? =

You can find details needed for testing purposes after you log in to your myPOS account and navigate to Online > Shopping carts > WooCommerce. Other option is to visit WooCommerce integration page at https://www.mypos.eu/en/platforms/woocommerce-pos.
